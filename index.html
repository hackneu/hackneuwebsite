About HackNEU 2017

2017 is an inaugural year for Northeastern’s Hackathon “Hack NEU”. This hackathon will challenge Northeastern’s Graduate and PHD students from different streams to come together and work on challenges in their daytoday lives. With the ever evolving technologies you can experience things you have never done or seen before. VR/AR is taking world on new adventures and we want you to experiment with this and many more new techs to generate ideas and develop them into a real life products as part of this hackathon experience. Hackathons are great to work on projects with people you may or maynot have met before to build something awesome.
So join us in this adventure and be the change you want to see it the world.

Spring 2017 Theme: “Hack the New!”


VR/AR, Artificial Intelligence, Machine learning, Data science, IoT etc. Basically all things on the cutting edge of technology!
